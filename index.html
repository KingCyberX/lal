Be a Volunteer

Shahbaz Qalandar Foundation aims to serve legacy of enlightenment to serve Humanity, propagated by Laal Shahbaz Qalandar.

You are requested to join us and give your hands to achieve the following goals:

  • Revival and Revitalization of National Art and Culture such as Islamic Calligraphy and Persian language.

  • Validation and Translation of his poetry in various Regional , National and International languages to propagate his Message of Humanity.

  • Authentic Biography and Documentary on his life "Qalandar's journey from Persia to Sehwan".

  • Establishment of "Mastam" Music Studio where Sufi Kalam will be enchanted in various languages.

  • Establishment of "Institute of Clinical Psychology " for the Rehabilitation and Mental well being of Drug Addicts mendicants , Prostitutes and Transgender Community.

  • Establishment of Museum where Holy Relics of Laal Shahbaz Qalandar will be preserved and Displayed.

  • Establishment of a language Learning center where Sufi Poetry will be taught and will be preserved as our cultural legacy for future generations.

  • Development of Spiritual Sufi Raqs genre.

  • Foundation of a Digital Library in Sehwan where e Library will serve the International researchers to promote research on Our Culture and Art in general and Culture of Sehwan in particular.

  • Establishment of Research Center i.e. Language and Research Lab for Documentation and Preservation of our Regional languages and Culture.

  • Foundation of University in Sehwan.

  • Development of a Channel in Sehwan.
